Usage context — Accordion
What is Startpage?
Startpage is the world's most private search engine — it delivers Google results without any of the tracking, ads, or profiling.
How does Anonymous View work?
Anonymous View lets you browse websites through Startpage's servers, so the site you visit never sees your real IP address or any identifying information.
Is Startpage free to use?
Yes, Startpage is completely free. It is supported by showing a small number of clearly-labelled private ads alongside search results.
